Everyday people meet to discuss issues and challenges. Such discussions could take the form of conferences, symposiums or talk shows. For instance, a company could be meeting for an annual sales conference; a board could be meeting to discuss strategy or budget; a panel discussion could be held by local business persons and wananchi to discuss the way forward for the county; maybe even your church planning committee could be meeting to discuss whether to start a school or how to grow the existing fledgling one; it could be a focus group put together to obtain feedback ahead of rolling out a prototype or just a brainstorming session.

All such fora require a leader with specific skills set. This leader must remain neutral throughout the forum. He must be someone who can clearly and concisely present the topic of discussion, encourage participation from everyone while handling challenging situations, control the meeting, keep it focused and assist members arrive at a decision if that’s what the forum calls for; in other instances, the facilitator simply sees to it that the issue or problem is discussed. We offer such facilitation.
